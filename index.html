Open For Takeout and Local Delivery

In light of recent events and restrictions, Cinque Terre will remain open for takeout and local delivery only. Although it is a frightening and uncertain time for our planet, we plan to continue to do what we do best: make delicious Italian food. Health and Safety in food is always our top priority, and we have been working harder than ever to enforce strict hygiene and preparation standards throughout our entire establishment. The best we can do for each other during this time is to take care of ourselves. Wash your hands, keep your distance and be safe. But don't be hungry ;)

Orders are available from all of our menus, including our 3 Course Menu and Bar Menu. Call us to see if you meet our delivery requirements. Feeding the whole family? Check out our Family Dinner Menu
!

Our Story

From a humble beginning in Manhattan's Upper East Side named Firenze to Verona Ristorante in Farmingdale, Anthony and Pam Page have established themselves as seasoned restauranteurs. Their passion for creating an authentic and unforgettable dining experience, along with their warm hospitality, is at the heart of this couple's third and grandest venture.
